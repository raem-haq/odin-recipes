Easy Lasagna Recipe

Simple and quick lasagna | Serves 1 | 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Frozen Lasagna
  • 20g Shredded Cheese

Instruction

  1. Ensure cheese is at room temperature before cooking.
  2. Microwave lasagna for 6 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle Chedder.
  4. Let it cool for 2 minute.
  5. Serve
